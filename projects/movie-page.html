French Revolution movie-page

The title of this project is "The French Revolution- Independence from the Ashes." It is about the story of Alexandre Eric. He is an abandoned teenager left on the streets of Paris with no family to care for him. He always got teased by his neighbors and others. And because of seeing King Louis XVI is treating the poors very badly, he wants to become the next leader by doing good deeds and taking control over the citizens.

Content: I made a webpage about this fictional story--The French Revolution and the Independence from the Ashes.

Reflection: Something that is challenges is that where we want to place the image on between the text and the use of the the display properties. Some major takeaways is how we can use our own ideas to make a story of something that we like. We are able to use of knowledge and the basics of HTML and CSS and use it in a project. Some concepts in HTML that we used are lists, paraagraphs, heading, div&span, and forms. This helped me consolidate my knowledge of HTML and how each properties were used. In addition, some concepts that we use from the CSS are text-align, color, font-family, font-size, paddings & margin, background color, text-decoration, and float. Next step that I want to take is to go to do mor epractice in using these properties, to help me improve myself when we are starting to learn advance web designs of HTML and CSS.
